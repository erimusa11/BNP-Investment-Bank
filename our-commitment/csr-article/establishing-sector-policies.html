Our sector policies

To regulate BNP Paribas Fortis' financing and investment activities in some sensitive areas, we implemented sector policies defining the conditions under which the bank may complete transactions. To this day, there are eight sector policies:

  • Palm oil
  • Defence
  • Nuclear energy
  • Wood pulp
  • Coal fired power generation
  • Mining
  • Agriculture
  • Unconventional oil & gas 

Public disclosures:

  • Goods and activities excluded
  • Tobacco
