THE EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE

The Executive Committee of BNP Paribas Fortis NV/SA consists of 13 members: 6 members of the Executive Board in their respective roles and 7 heads of businesses or support services. The Executive Committee (ExCo) is responsible for the execution of BNP Paribas Fortis strategy and policy.
