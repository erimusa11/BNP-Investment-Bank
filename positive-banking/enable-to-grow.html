  • Positive Banking

You can count on our team to help you grow and make a success of your projects. Looking for a loan to buy your very first place? Or a financial partner who will help you to sketch out the future of your company? We’ll get to work on it together. We also give less well-off people a leg-up through social solidarity initiatives or tailor-made financing solutions. Together we can create a successful future.



  • Together we’ll help you grow

    Our aim is to help you carry out your plans and projects. Not only do we provide loans, we’ve developed an Innovation Hub specifically for start-ups and scale-ups, we have special Starter Advisors for new company founders and we run digital workshops for businesspeople. As your bank we want to work hand-in-hand with you to achieve positive growth.

  • A caring bank

    As the bank for a changing world, we’re right at the heart of society. Through BNP Paribas Fortis Foundation we provide assistance to vulnerable sections of the population and support to social projects. Moreover, our microStart subsidiary is there to help micro-business owners with both loans and coaching.
