AN INTERNSHIP AT BNP PARIBAS FORTIS

Get started with your future career with us

If you want to gain an insight into what a future career in the role would be like, meaningful tasks, constant dialogue and responsibilities are essential. That’s perfect, as that’s exactly what we can offer you. By joining BNP Paribas Fortis, you will discover the business world and learn about different positions within our bank’s various entities. More importantly: as we are a learning organisation, the tasks that you are assigned will help you to develop your skills and expand your knowledge by being made a central part of a team of professionals. All of this will be overseen by an internship mentor. What’s more, you will meet people. And thanks to the size of our organisation, you will have many opportunities to do so. It’s your chance to make connections and expand your network.

Fancy joining us?

You have chosen BNP Paribas Fortis to get started with your future career. Thank you! We’re delighted to hear that. Below is an overview of the eligibility conditions for people wishing to join us:

  • Your internship must fall within your study programme (third year of a Bachelor’s degree or a Master’s degree).
  • If you were planning on doing a work placement with us, we need you to provide an internship agreement signed by your school or university.
  • We welcome interns from September to December, January to May and June to August.
  • In order to get an internship, we want to find out why you would like to do a placement with us. You will therefore need to submit an application.

All internships are unpaid. However, you will receive a daily fixed allowance to cover your transport costs and meals.

Learn more about our available internships

Recruitment process

Meet the criteria? Perfect! You may now begin the recruitment process and just need to get through a number of stages.

Application

Select the internship that best matches your skills and aspirations, and send us your application online at least three months before it starts. A quick tip: make sure that you put your cover letter and CV together carefully to improve your chances of being selected. Provide suitable arguments to persuade us to choose you.

Next

Preselection

The HR teams will assess your application. If you are preselected, your CV will be forwarded to the manager for the entity in question.

Next

Interview

If your application caught the attention of the manager for the entity in question, you will be invited to a selection interview. A quick tip: prepare carefully and show that you are interested. For example, find out about our company, its strategy and its values. Try to set yourself apart.

Next

Contract

Well done! You stood out and impressed us. You have been selected. An internship agreement will therefore be sent to your school or university for them to sign. It’s then over to you!

Useful information

BNP Paribas Fortis takes part in many student fairs throughout Belgium. Come and meet our employees and discuss the opportunities that we can offer. It’s also a chance to learn more about us and discover our employer promise.
